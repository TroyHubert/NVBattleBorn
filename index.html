The Draw



  • No State Income Tax

    • One of the only states in the US to not have a state income tax.

    Businesses and their staff have relocated in droves. Since 2010, Nevada has grown by over 15%!3


  • Mining

    • Considered one of the most important states for mining gold and silver.

    Home of the Carlin Trend, one of the world's largest districts for mining gold.


  • It's Only Natural

    • A lesser known factoid is how mountainous the state of Nevada is; it's actually in the top 10 states with a high elevation.

    From its hiking trails, to its photogenic scenery and sunsets; Nevada has what it takes to keep the great outdoors a short drive away.


The Facts



  • Roots in Spain and Mexico

    • First claimed by Spaniards in the 1500's, ownership fell under Mexico after they gained independence in 1821.4

    The United States secured the land from Mexico in 1848, during the Mexican-American war.4


  • Gold, Silver, and Blood of Brothers

    • Four years after the height of the gold rush, silver was discovered in 1858.5

    In 1864, during the waning moments of the Civil War, Nevada joined the Union and became the Battle Born State.6


  • These Colors Don't Bleed

    • The state flag features one primary color and 3 contrasting, dynamic, and supporting colors.

    Blue, White, Gold, and Green respectively stand for vigilance, loyalty and justice, wealth and generosity, and earth and fertility.7


The Fun



  • Ridin' Birdie

    • With it's year round warm climate, Nevada is home to 70 golf courses: some of which are TPC facilities visited on PGA tours.


  • Keeping Things Blue

    • After appreciating the view, bathing in the sun, and the humble mountain town vibes; Spend a summer night watching Shakespeare performed on a lakeside theater.


  • Bustling Night Life

    • From illustrious gaming resorts to an emergent art scene, Nevada has much to offer. When you're done, book a night at one of many 4 and 5 star hotels and enjoy some incredible cuisine.

    If none of that is a selling point, we have All You Can Eat Sushi.
