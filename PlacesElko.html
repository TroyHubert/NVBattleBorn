Cowboy Up

  • Basque in the Culture

    • Elko has a strong basque culture thanks to seven decades of immigrants and descendants.31

    Annually around the 4th of July weekend, the National Basque festival has hearty meals, heavy stone lifting and other feats of strength competitions, handball games, and 5k fun runs.31

  • Mural Mosey

    • Started in 2019; 40 artists painted 51 murals on 30 walls.32

    These vibrant works capture and reflect Basque and Native American heritage.32

  • Heli-Yeah!

    • Take a helicopter ride to the highest peaks of the Ruby Mountains and jump out for a life changing ski experience.
